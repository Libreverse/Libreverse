What is Libreverse?

Libreverse is a social platform that puts your privacy and control at the center of the experience. Unlike traditional social media platforms that profit from your data, Libreverse is built on the principles of:

  • Privacy by Design - Your data belongs to you, not us
  • User Empowerment - You control what you share and with whom
  • Meaningful Connections - Quality over quantity in social interactions
  • Transparency - Open-source development and clear policies
