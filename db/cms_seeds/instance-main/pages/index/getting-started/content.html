Welcome to Libreverse! This comprehensive guide will walk you through setting up your account, understanding our privacy features, and making the most of our platform.

Step 1: Creating Your Account

Getting started with Libreverse is simple and privacy-focused from the very beginning:

  1. Visit our registration page - Head to /create-account
  2. Choose your authentication method - You can create an account with just an email address
  3. Set a strong password - We recommend using a password manager
  4. Verify your email - Check your inbox for a verification link

Step 2: Understanding Privacy Settings

Libreverse gives you granular control over your privacy. Here are the key settings to configure:

Profile Visibility

  • Public - Your profile is visible to everyone
  • Community - Only visible to registered Libreverse users
  • Private - Only visible to people you've connected with

Experience Sharing

When you share an experience, you can set its visibility level:

  • Public - Visible to everyone, including search engines
  • Unlisted - Accessible via direct link but not publicly searchable
  • Community Only - Only visible to registered users
  • Private - Only visible to you

Step 3: Creating Your First Experience

Experiences are the heart of Libreverse - they're how you share meaningful moments, insights, and stories:

  1. Navigate to the dashboard - Click "Create Experience" from your dashboard
  2. Choose a compelling title - Make it descriptive and engaging
  3. Write your content - Share your story, insight, or experience
  4. Add tags - Help others discover your content
  5. Set privacy level - Choose who can see your experience
  6. Publish - Share your experience with the community

Step 4: Connecting with Others

Building meaningful connections is what Libreverse is all about:

Discovery

  • Use the search function to find experiences that interest you
  • Browse by tags and categories
  • Check out trending experiences

Engagement

  • React to experiences that resonate with you
  • Leave thoughtful comments
  • Share experiences you find valuable

Step 5: Advanced Features

Federation

Libreverse supports ActivityPub federation, allowing you to connect with users on other compatible platforms like Mastodon, PeerTube, and others.

API Access

Developers can integrate with Libreverse using our REST API and GraphQL endpoints. Check out our API documentation to get started.

Data Export

Your data belongs to you. At any time, you can export all your data from your account settings.
