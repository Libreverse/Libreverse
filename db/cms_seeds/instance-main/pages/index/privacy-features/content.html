üîí Privacy by Design

Unlike platforms that retrofit privacy features as an afterthought, Libreverse was built from the ground up with privacy as a core principle. Every feature, every decision, and every line of code considers your privacy first.

Key Privacy Features

1. Granular Privacy Controls

You have complete control over who sees your content:

  • Public - Visible to everyone, including search engines
  • Unlisted - Accessible via direct link but not searchable
  • Community Only - Visible only to registered Libreverse users
  • Private - Only visible to you
  • Custom Groups - Share with specific people you choose

2. Guest Mode for Anonymous Browsing

Our unique guest mode allows you to explore Libreverse without creating an account or leaving digital traces. Guest sessions are:

  • Completely anonymous
  • Not tracked across sessions
  • Automatically deleted after inactivity
  • Convertible to full accounts if desired

3. Data Minimization

We only collect data that's absolutely necessary for the platform to function:

  • No tracking cookies for advertising
  • No data mining for algorithmic manipulation
  • No selling of user data to third parties
  • No unnecessary telemetry or analytics

4. End-to-End Encryption

Sensitive communications are protected with state-of-the-art encryption:

  • Private messages are encrypted before leaving your device
  • Only you and the recipient can read the content
  • Even Libreverse administrators cannot access encrypted content
  • Encryption keys are managed locally on your device
